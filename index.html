QUESTIONS

A few things you might be wondering.

Why is The Positioning Breakdown free?

Because before recommending experimentation, we first want to understand whether experimentation is actually the right next step.

Do I need to prepare anything?

No. Bring your homepage, messaging, offer or simply the decision that's been occupying your team's attention.

Will we redesign my homepage during the session?

Probably not. Our goal isn't to redesign. It's to understand whether the homepage is actually where the problem starts.

What is the Opportunity Brief?

It's a one-page summary of what we uncovered together, including the strongest opportunity, the reasoning behind it and whether it deserves experimentation.

Can anyone book a Decision Session?

No. Decision Sessions are only recommended when The Positioning Breakdown uncovers an opportunity that would benefit from structured experimentation.

How do I know if The Positioning Breakdown is right for my team?

If your team keeps revisiting the same marketing decisions— headlines, messaging, positioning, homepage, pricing or launch strategy— The Positioning Breakdown helps determine whether you're solving the right problem before investing more time or resources.
